# 📌 Overview

TransitOps AI is a centralized platform that enables organizations to efficiently manage their transportation operations through an intelligent dashboard, real-time fleet monitoring, automated workflows, and analytics-driven insights.

The platform replaces traditional spreadsheets and manual logbooks with a digital command center for logistics and fleet operations.

# 🚀 Key Business Rules

✅ Vehicle Registration Number must be unique.

✅ Cargo weight cannot exceed vehicle capacity.

✅ Drivers with expired licenses cannot be assigned.

✅ Vehicles in maintenance cannot be dispatched.

✅ Driver or Vehicle already on a trip cannot be reassigned.

✅ Dispatching a trip automatically updates statuses.

✅ Completing a trip restores availability.
